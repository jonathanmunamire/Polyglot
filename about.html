POLYGLOT LANGUAGES SCHOOL
KIGALI (RWANDA)

Polyglot Language School is a language school for all the people interested and passionate in becoming polyglots.
Polyglot Language School was created in 2022 by Mr Jonathan Amani Munamire who is himself a Congolese who found it difficult to adapt in another country because of language barriers.
That's why Polyglot Language School exists! We are here to help all passionate people to go beyond their limits and to tear down all the languages barriers they may have.
With all flexible programs, you can choose a program that meets your expectations!
We have more than 45 certified teachers and tutors; with a lot of experience. We hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a strong community of learners.
